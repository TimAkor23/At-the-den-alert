Emergency Protocols

Fire Emergency

Steps to follow in case of fire

  1. Activate nearest fire alarm
  2. Evacuate using designated routes
  3. Assemble at football field
  4. Account for all students

Lockdown Procedure

Steps for security lockdown

  1. Lock all doors and windows
  2. Move students out of sight
  3. Silence all devices
  4. Wait for all-clear signal

Medical Emergency

Responding to health crises

  1. Call school nurse (ext. 555)
  2. Administer first aid if trained
  3. Clear area around patient
  4. Await medical professionals
